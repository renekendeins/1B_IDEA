Idea generator

What is IDEA GENERATOR?

Idea Generator is a simple app that it allows you to create ideas from words chosen by you which may be related to each other

Multiple choices

You can develop an idea from a single word or from more than one. These can be chosen by you or randomly

And then?

Once you have an idea we will offer you a table in which you can develop in multiple ways, taking into account different points of view
